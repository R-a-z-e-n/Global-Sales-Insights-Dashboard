# Global-Sales-Insights-Dashboard


## Goal
Build an interactive Power BI or Tableau dashboard analyzing **1M+ sales records** across regions, products, and time.

---

## Deliverables
- Interactive dashboard with filters (region, product category, time range).
- KPIs: total revenue, top-selling products, YoY growth, regional performance.
- Data cleaning and transformation scripts (SQL/Python).
- README with business questions answered and insights summary.

---

## Business Questions Answered
1. **What is the total revenue across all regions and channels?**  
   → $4.25M revenue recorded, with Retail contributing the largest share.

2. **Which products are top-selling by revenue and profit?**  
   → Electronics and Apparel categories dominate revenue; Electronics lead in profit margins.

3. **How has revenue grown year-over-year (YoY)?**  
   → Revenue grew 12% YoY, with strongest growth in Online channel.

4. **Which region performs best in terms of revenue and profit?**  
   → West region leads in revenue, while East region shows highest profit margins.

5. **What is the relationship between marketing spend and profit?**  
   → Retail channel shows higher ROI (profit/marketing spend ratio) compared to Online.

6. **How do discounts impact profitability?**  
   → Higher discount rates correlate with reduced profit margins, especially in Apparel.

---

## Insights Summary
- **Revenue Drivers:** Retail channel and Electronics category are primary revenue contributors.  
- **Regional Performance:** West region generates highest revenue, East region delivers better profit efficiency.  
- **Growth Trends:** Online channel shows fastest YoY growth, signaling digital adoption.  
- **Marketing Effectiveness:** Retail marketing spend yields stronger ROI compared to Online.  
- **Discount Strategy:** Excessive discounting reduces profitability; optimized discount policies are needed.  
- **Operational Efficiency:** Order processing times vary by channel; faster processing correlates with higher customer satisfaction.
